Our Story

Founded in 2015 by fitness enthusiasts and industry veterans, FitGear Pro was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to make premium gym equipment accessible to everyone, from home fitness enthusiasts to commercial gym owners.

What started as a small warehouse operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in fitness equipment supply. We've equipped over 2,500 gyms, helped 50,000+ individuals create their perfect home gyms, and built lasting relationships with leading equipment manufacturers worldwide.
